Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. SurAxisTech uses cookies to improve performance, understand usage patterns, and provide a smoother browsing experience.

01

How We Use Cookies

  • Keep essential website functions working reliably
  • Measure traffic and understand how visitors use pages and forms
  • Improve speed, usability, and overall website quality
  • Support security and anti-abuse measures
02

Types of Cookies

Essential Cookies

Required for core site functionality such as navigation, forms, and security features.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand visitor behavior so we can improve content, performance, and user experience.

Preference Cookies

Remember selected settings to provide a more consistent experience across visits.

03

Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that blocking certain cookies may impact functionality or reduce website performance.

Most browsers let you delete stored cookies, block future cookies, or notify you before a cookie is set.

04

Updates

We may update this Cookies Policy when tools, regulations, or website functionality change. Any significant updates will be reflected by the revised date on this page.
