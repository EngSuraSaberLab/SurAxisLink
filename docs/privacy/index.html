Privacy Policy

How SurAxisTech collects, uses, and protects your information across our website and project inquiry process.

SurAxisTech respects your privacy and handles personal data with care. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it when you use our website or submit a project inquiry.

01

Information We Collect

When you submit our project inquiry form, we may collect:

  • Your full name
  • Email address
  • Phone or WhatsApp number
  • Project details, goals, budget range, and timeline information

We may also collect technical usage data such as browser type, device type, and general interaction data through cookies and analytics tools.

02

How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Review and respond to project inquiries
  • Contact you through email or WhatsApp when relevant to your request
  • Prepare suitable proposals and technical recommendations
  • Improve website experience, service quality, and operational performance

We do not sell your personal data. We only use it for legitimate business and communication purposes.

03

Data Protection

We apply practical technical and organizational safeguards to protect submitted information against unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. Access to inquiry data is limited to authorized team members.

While no online system can guarantee absolute security, we continuously improve our security practices and review our processes regularly.

04

Third-Party Services

Our website may rely on trusted third-party tools for security, analytics, and infrastructure support. These providers process data only as needed to deliver their services.

If third-party links appear on our site, each external platform has its own privacy policy and practices.

05

User Rights

You may request to:

  • Access the personal data you shared with us
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your inquiry data where applicable
  • Withdraw communication consent for future follow-up messages
06

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, contact us and our team will assist you.
